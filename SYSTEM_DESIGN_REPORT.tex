\section{What is UniLodge v2?}

\begin{tcolorbox}[infoblue, title={\faBuilding\quad Platform Summary}]
\textbf{UniLodge v2} is a production-ready, enterprise-grade accommodation management platform
designed specifically for academic institutions. It connects students and visiting scholars with
verified room listings across universities, offering a full digital lifecycle from discovery to
booking and check-out.
\end{tcolorbox}

\vspace{0.5cm}
The platform is built on a modern three-tier architecture combining a \textcolor{AccentBlue}{Next.js 14}
frontend, an \textcolor{AccentGreen}{Express.js} REST API backend, and a \textcolor{AccentPurple}{MongoDB Atlas}
cloud database — all glued together with \textcolor{AccentOrange}{AI-powered features} via the OpenRouter API.

Email Normaliz.    & Lowercase normalization prevents duplicate accounts     & Best Practice \\
HTTPS/TLS          & Mandatory in production environment                    & PCI DSS \\
